# DineLead - Landing Page Proposal

## 1. Storytelling Elements

### Hero Section — The Hook

**Headline:** "Turn Every Restaurant Into a Business Opportunity"  
**Subheadline:** "DineLead automatically finds, organizes, and helps you reach out to restaurant leads — so you can focus on closing deals, not hunting for contacts."

**Visual concept:** A clean dashboard screenshot or animated illustration showing the flow: map pins appearing on a map -> data flowing into a CRM table -> an email being sent.

---

### The Story Arc (scroll-based narrative)

The landing page follows a **3-act storytelling structure** as the user scrolls:

**Act 1 — The Struggle (Problem)**  
A short relatable narrative about the pain:
> "You spend hours Googling restaurants, copying phone numbers into spreadsheets, writing the same cold email over and over. By the time you've built a list of 50 leads, the day is gone — and you haven't made a single call."

**Act 2 — The Turning Point (Solution)**  
Introduce DineLead as the shift:
> "What if you could scrape hundreds of restaurant leads from any city in minutes, manage them in one place, and let AI write your outreach emails?"

**Act 3 — The New Reality (Features + Proof)**  
Walk through features with benefit-first copy, ending with a CTA.

---

## 2. The Problem Addressed

### Section Title: "The Restaurant Lead Problem"

Present **3-4 pain points** as cards or illustrated blocks:

| Pain Point | Description |
|---|---|
| **Manual data collection is slow** | Searching Google Maps, copying names, addresses, phone numbers one by one. It takes hours to build even a small lead list. |
| **Spreadsheets don't scale** | Once you have 100+ leads, spreadsheets become chaotic. No status tracking, no activity history, no search filters. |
| **Cold outreach is repetitive** | Writing personalized emails for each restaurant is time-consuming. Copy-paste templates feel generic and get ignored. |
| **No visibility into your pipeline** | You can't see how many leads are prospects vs. contacted vs. closed. There's no dashboard, no conversion rate, no way to measure progress. |

**Tone:** Empathetic, not aggressive. "We've been there" energy.

---

## 3. The Solution Offered

### Section Title: "One Platform. From Scraping to Closing."

Present the solution as a **3-step flow** (visual diagram or numbered cards):

```
Step 1: SCRAPE          Step 2: MANAGE          Step 3: CLOSE
Pick a city &     ->    Filter, track, &   ->   AI-generated emails
radius. DineLead        add notes to every       & activity logging
finds restaurants        lead in your CRM.       to seal the deal.
automatically.
```

**Key message:** DineLead replaces your fragmented workflow (Google Maps + spreadsheets + email drafts) with one unified tool.

**Supporting copy:**
> "DineLead scrapes restaurant data from OpenStreetMap — no expensive API keys needed. Leads flow directly into your CRM where you can track status, log calls, and generate AI-powered outreach emails in seconds."

---

## 4. List of Key Features

### Section Title: "Everything You Need to Build & Close Your Pipeline"

Present as **6 feature cards** with icons:

| Feature | Title | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Automated Restaurant Scraping** | Enter a city and radius — DineLead scrapes restaurant names, addresses, phones, websites, ratings, and reviews from OpenStreetMap. No API keys. No cost per query. |
| 2 | **Smart CRM Pipeline** | Track every lead through Prospect -> Contacted -> Closed stages. Filter by status, rating, location, or industry. Bulk actions for efficiency. |
| 3 | **AI Email Generator** | Generate personalized cold outreach emails with one click. Choose your tone (professional, friendly, casual) and language (English or Indonesian). Powered by AI that knows each restaurant's details. |
| 4 | **Activity & Notes Tracking** | Log every call, email, meeting, and follow-up. Add notes to any lead. Full interaction history so you never lose context. |
| 5 | **Real-Time Dashboard** | See your total leads, conversion rate, average rating, and recent activity at a glance. Auto-refreshing stats so your numbers are always current. |
| 6 | **Flexible Data Export** | Export leads to Excel, CSV, or JSON. Choose exactly which fields to include. Filter by date range, status, or rating before exporting. |

## 6. Design Direction (Suggestions)

- **Color palette:** Dark navy/slate primary + vibrant accent (orange or emerald green) for CTAs
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif (Inter or similar)
- **Layout:** Full-width hero, alternating left-right feature sections, card-based pain points
- **Animations:** Subtle scroll-triggered fade-ins, no heavy motion
- **Mobile:** Fully responsive, stacked layout on mobile
- **Style:** Modern SaaS landing page — clean, professional, trustworthy
